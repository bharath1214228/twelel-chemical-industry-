    • Battery Chemicals

    Aiming to be a part of the change and development all around the world, we added cobalt and nickel salts to our product portfolio. These chemicals are widely used in battery production as cathode chemicals which play an important role in the production of the electric vehicle batteries and mobile electronic devices.  Moreover, these chemicals are also used in the metal plating industry. Based on our strong knowledge and experience in metal-based chemical production, we are able to produce such chemicals by targeting to keep both quality and customer satisfaction at the highest level. For more information, you can contact us at info@egekimya.com
